Attention Members and Guests!

Welcome to TC'S. Where all us regular people can come and hang out and have a good time. Sit back have a cigarette....that's right smoking indoors (what a concept). We are 21 and up only private club. No kids allowed. We have four bar style pool tables. We offer a Large stage with a full PA with an array of lights and effects for our live bands. We have live music every Friday and Saturday night and Open Mic on Wednesdays and Sundays. Serving a full menu from 11AM - 2AM with an incredible burger and you might even try a spam egg and cheese sandwich. A place for good food, good friends, and great music.
